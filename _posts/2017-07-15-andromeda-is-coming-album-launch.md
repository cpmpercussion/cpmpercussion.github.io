On 15 July 2017 I launched the debut self-titled album from **Andromeda is Coming** with a live performance at the Ainslie and Gorman Arts Centre in Canberra.

Andromeda is Coming is the improvised music project of [Dr Alexander Hunter](https://alexanderhunter.com.au) and me (Charles Martin). We play long-form improvisations and follow the sounds wherever they go. _Andromeda is Coming_ is our first record. [Ash Drury](https://www.instagram.com/ashdrury0/) supported us at the launch.
